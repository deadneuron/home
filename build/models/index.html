Models

A lot of interesting neural networks have been introduced over the years, but there is no place that organizes and discusses some of these fascinating models. This is a new take on the model zoo.

Perceptron (1958)

Frank Rosenblatt

The beginning of our great field of research.

Paper

Neocognitron (1979)

Kunihiko Fukushima

A lesser known piece of history. The first convolutional neural network. A breakthrough development for computer vision.

Paper

Hopfield Network (1982)

J. J. Hopfield

The first convolutional neural network. A breakthrough development for computer vision.

Paper

LeNet (1989)

Yann LeCun

Most popularly known for it's inclusion in the paper that introduced backpropagation. This convolutional architecture would become the foundation for the most powerful computer vision models of the future.
