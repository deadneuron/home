My Research

I'm really interested in exploring the inner workings of deep neural networks. My PhD research is about neural partitioning, which is the process of splitting deep neural networks into sparse subnetworks that can be trained, executed, or analyzed independently. My work touches on several powerful topics in machine learning including: low-cost ensemble learning, interpretability methods, network pruning, continual learning, neuroregeneration, evolutionary methods and quantum computation. Check out my research papers to learn more about what I'm working on.
